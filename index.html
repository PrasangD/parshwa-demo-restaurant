Our Story

Rooted in tradition, plated for today.

Saffron & Sage began in 2016 as a small family kitchen with an ambitious idea — that the food we grew up with deserved the ceremony of fine dining, without losing an ounce of its soul.

We source spices from a single-estate farm in Kerala, greens from growers across the Konkan coast, and seafood from the morning catch at Bhau Cha Dhakka. Everything is ground fresh, tempered to order, and served the way our grandmothers intended — with generosity.

The result is a menu that feels both familiar and quietly surprising: a Malvani curry beside a saffron-cured scallop, a nostalgic dal beside a smoked-beet chaat.

"Cook with memory. Plate with intent." — Chef Aarav Deshmukh, Founder

Meet the Chef

Chef Aarav Deshmukh

Trained in Mumbai and Lyon, Chef Aarav returned home with one mission: to give Dombivli a table worth travelling for. His cooking is precise, seasonal, and deeply personal — every plate a page from his family's kitchen diary.
